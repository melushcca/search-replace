Search & Replace

Case: We have a File with 1000 of Strings, let's say they are articlenames (ex. "Article Brand Model xy") Then you have another File with multiple brands. Now we want to replace all of our brands in the file with our articlenames (ex. result: "Article Model xy")

1. Step

Copy & Paste here the words you want to search and replace (the brands)

2. Step

Copy & Paste here the Text where you want the words be replaced in. (the articlenames)

3. Step

When you did Copy & Paste your data from the first and second Step click Start to run the Search & Replace programm.
