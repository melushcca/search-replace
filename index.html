Search & Replace

Case: You have 1 File with 1000 of Strings, let's say they are articlenames (ex. "Article Brand Model xy") Then you have an other File with multiple Brands and you want to replace the brands in your articlename by nothing ('').

1. Step

Copy & Paste here the words you want to search and replace (ex. brands)

2. Step

Copy & Paste here the Text where you want the words be replaced in. (ex. articlenames)

3. Step

When you did Copy & Paste your data from the first and second Step click Start to run the Search & Replace programm.
